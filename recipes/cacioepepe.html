Cacio E Pepe!

This is a recipe for Cacio E Pepe, a Roman speciality from the county of Lazio in Italy. Cacio e pepe literally translates to "Cheese and Pepper", which is an apt name for this dish. It is simple to make but difficult to master. As with all simple Italian dishes, the key to success is in the ingredients. High quality ingredients are of paramount importance when making a dish with only 2-3 elements. There is no way hide poor quality in similar style dishes like pomodoro al basilico or aglio e oglio.

Ingredients

  • Pasta (Egg pastas work the best, ex taglionlini)
  • Unsalted butter
  • Grana Padano or Parmesan
  • Freshly Cracked Black Pepper
  • Pecorino Romano Cheese
